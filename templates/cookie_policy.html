What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website.

Cookies help websites:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Keep you signed in
  • Improve website performance
  • Enhance security
  • Understand website usage
  • Personalize your experience

Cookies do not typically contain information that directly identifies you. However, they may be associated with information you provide while using our Services.

Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, Work-A-Beez may use similar technologies including:

  • Local Storage
  • Session Storage
  • Web Beacons
  • Pixels
  • Software Development Kits (SDKs)
  • Log Files
  • Browser Storage

These technologies help provide functionality similar to cookies.

Why We Use Cookies

Cookies help us operate the Work-A-Beez platform securely and efficiently.

We use cookies to:

  • Authenticate users
  • Maintain secure login sessions
  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Remember user preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Measure website usage
  • Diagnose technical issues
  • Improve customer experience
  • Support platform security

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the platform to function.

Examples include:

  • User authentication
  • Session management
  • Security validation
  • Load balancing
  • CSRF protection
  • Account access

Without these cookies, certain Services cannot function properly.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and improve usability.

Examples include:

  • Language selection
  • Theme preferences
  • Time zone
  • Dashboard layout
  • Notification preferences
  • Accessibility settings

Functional cookies improve convenience but are not strictly required for basic functionality.

Performance Cookies

Performance cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Services.

Examples include:

  • Page performance
  • Feature usage
  • Navigation paths
  • Error reporting
  • Response times
  • Device compatibility

Information collected through performance cookies is generally aggregated and used to improve platform performance.

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us better understand how our Services are used.

Examples include:

  • Most visited pages
  • Session duration
  • Device types
  • Browser versions
  • Geographic regions (generalized)
  • Traffic sources

Analytics information helps improve future product development.

Security Cookies

Security cookies help protect customer accounts.

Examples include:

  • Authentication tokens
  • Login validation
  • Session verification
  • Fraud detection
  • Suspicious activity monitoring
  • Account protection

Security cookies are essential to protecting customer information.

Cookies We Do Not Use

Unless specifically disclosed, Work-A-Beez does not use cookies for:

  • Selling personal information
  • Building advertising profiles
  • Behavioral advertising
  • Cross-site advertising without notice

Should our practices change, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Third-Party Cookies

Certain third-party providers integrated with Work-A-Beez may place cookies.

Examples may include:

  • Payment processors
  • Customer support platforms
  • Analytics providers
  • Identity providers
  • Embedded video providers
  • Documentation platforms

These providers maintain their own privacy and cookie policies.

Work-A-Beez does not control third-party cookies.

Session Cookies

Session cookies remain active only while your browser is open.

These cookies help:

  • Keep you signed in
  • Navigate securely
  • Prevent session hijacking
  • Maintain application state

Session cookies are deleted when your browser session ends.

Persistent Cookies

Persistent cookies remain on your device for a specified period or until deleted.

They help remember:

  • User preferences
  • Language settings
  • Accessibility options
  • Trusted devices
  • Login preferences (where enabled)

Persistent cookies automatically expire after their designated lifespan.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to:

  • View stored cookies
  • Delete cookies
  • Block cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Configure cookie preferences

Please note that disabling certain cookies may reduce functionality or prevent access to portions of the Services.

Browser Instructions

Instructions for managing cookies can usually be found within your browser settings.

Popular browsers include:

  • Microsoft Edge
  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari

Refer to your browser's documentation for specific instructions.

Cookie Consent

Where required by applicable law, Work-A-Beez may request your consent before placing certain non-essential cookies on your device.

You may modify your cookie preferences at any time, subject to browser limitations.

Do Not Track (DNT)

Some web browsers provide a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting.

Because there is currently no universally accepted standard for responding to DNT signals, Work-A-Beez may not respond to all DNT requests.

We continue to monitor developments regarding browser privacy standards.
