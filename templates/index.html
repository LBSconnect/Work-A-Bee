Frequently Asked Questions

Is Work-A-Bee free to use?

Work-A-Bee is free for your first 90 days on any plan, no credit card required. After that, pricing depends on your team size — see our Pricing page for details.

How does employee clock-in work?

Employees clock in and out from a simple PIN-based screen on office computers your admin authorizes as trusted devices, which helps prevent buddy punching and time theft.

Can Work-A-Bee handle payroll for hourly and contract workers?

Yes. Work-A-Bee tracks hours for both employees and contractors, calculates pay based on your rates and overtime rules, and generates weekly payroll reports you can email directly to your team.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Work-A-Bee runs entirely in your browser — no downloads, no hardware, no setup beyond registering the computers you want employees to clock in from.

Is my company's data secure?

Yes. Only devices your admin explicitly registers can be used to clock in or out, and each company's data is kept isolated from other businesses using Work-A-Bee.
