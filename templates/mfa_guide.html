Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) Guide

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is a planned enhancement to Work-A-Beez account security. It is not yet available.

Today, Work-A-Beez accounts are secured with a username/email and password. See the Password Policy for current requirements and the Security Overview for a broader look at how account access is protected.

This page will be updated with enrollment and usage instructions once MFA is released.
