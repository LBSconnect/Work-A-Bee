Welcome to Work-A-Beez

Work-A-Beez is your organization's workforce management platform.

Using Work-A-Beez you can:

  • Clock in and out
  • View your work schedule
  • Request time off
  • View PTO balances
  • Swap shifts
  • Pick up open shifts
  • Update availability
  • Receive company announcements
  • Complete assigned tasks
  • View employment documents
  • Update personal information
  • Communicate with supervisors

The features available to you depend on your organization's configuration and your assigned permissions.

System Requirements

Work-A-Beez supports most modern devices.

Supported platforms include:

  • Windows
  • macOS
  • Linux
  • iPhone (iOS)
  • Android
  • Tablets

Supported browsers include:

  • Google Chrome
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari

For the best experience, keep your browser and operating system up to date.

Getting Started

When your employer creates your Work-A-Beez account, you will receive an invitation email.

The invitation contains:

  • Your username or email address
  • A secure activation link
  • Instructions for creating your password
  • A link to the Work-A-Beez login page

If your invitation expires, contact your supervisor or administrator to request a new invitation.

Logging In

To access Work-A-Beez:

  1. Open your organization's Work-A-Beez portal.
  2. Enter your email address or username.
  3. Enter your password.
  4. Complete Multi-Factor Authentication (if required).
  5. Select Sign In.

If authentication is successful, you will be taken to your Employee Dashboard.

Employee Dashboard

The Employee Dashboard provides an overview of your workday.

Depending on your organization's configuration, your dashboard may include:

  • Current Clock Status
  • Today's Shift
  • Upcoming Schedule
  • Weekly Hours Worked
  • PTO Balance
  • Recent Notifications
  • Pending Requests
  • Company Announcements
  • Available Open Shifts
  • Assigned Tasks

Dashboard widgets may be customized by your employer.

Navigation

The main navigation menu typically includes:

  • Dashboard
  • Time Clock
  • Schedule
  • Leave
  • Availability
  • Marketplace
  • Messages
  • Documents
  • Profile
  • Settings

The available menu items may vary based on your role and permissions.

My Profile

Your employee profile contains your personal and employment information.

You may be able to view or update:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Mailing Address
  • Emergency Contact
  • Profile Photo
  • Preferred Language
  • Notification Preferences

Some information, such as your job title or department, may only be editable by your employer.

Updating Your Profile

To update your profile:

  1. Select Profile.
  2. Choose Edit Profile.
  3. Update the desired information.
  4. Select Save Changes.

Certain updates may require approval before becoming effective.

Password Management

To change your password:

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Select Security.
  3. Choose Change Password.
  4. Enter your current password.
  5. Create a new password.
  6. Confirm your new password.
  7. Save your changes.

For security, choose a strong, unique password that is not used for other accounts.

Multi-Factor Authentication

If your employer requires Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), you will be prompted to verify your identity during sign-in.

Depending on your organization's configuration, verification may occur through:

  • Authentication App
  • SMS Code
  • Email Verification
  • Security Key

MFA helps protect your account from unauthorized access.

Session Management

For your protection, Work-A-Beez may automatically sign you out after a period of inactivity.

Always sign out when using a shared or public computer.

Accessibility

Work-A-Beez is designed to support a wide range of users and assistive technologies.

Accessibility features may include:

  • Keyboard navigation
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Adjustable text scaling
  • High-contrast support
  • Responsive layouts
  • Accessible form controls

Accessibility options may vary depending on browser and device capabilities.

Getting Help

If you experience issues logging in or accessing your account:

  • Contact your supervisor.
  • Contact your organization's Work-A-Beez Administrator.
  • Use your organization's designated support process.
  • Refer to the troubleshooting section later in this guide.
