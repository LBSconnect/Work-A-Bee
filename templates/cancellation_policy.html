Welcome to the Work-A-Beez Cancellation Policy.

This policy explains how organizations may cancel their Work-A-Beez subscription, what happens after cancellation, how billing is affected, and how customer data is managed following service termination.

Customers are encouraged to review this policy together with the Subscription FAQ, Billing FAQ, Refund Policy, and Service Level Agreement (SLA).

Purpose

This policy establishes the standard process for:

  • Cancelling subscriptions
  • Ending service
  • Managing customer data
  • Clarifying billing responsibilities
  • Explaining post-cancellation procedures

It is intended to provide transparency and ensure a predictable transition when a customer chooses to discontinue the service.

Scope

This policy applies to:

  • Monthly subscriptions
  • Annual subscriptions
  • Multi-year agreements
  • Enterprise subscriptions

Certain contractual agreements may contain terms that differ from this standard policy.

Where a signed agreement exists, the agreement governs.

Requesting Cancellation

Authorized organization representatives may request cancellation.

Typically, authorized individuals include:

  • Billing Administrators
  • Organization Administrators
  • Contract Owners
  • Authorized Purchasing Representatives

Cancellation requests may be submitted through:

  • Customer Support
  • Customer Success
  • Billing Portal (where available)
  • Written notice as required by the service agreement

Identity verification may be required before processing a cancellation request.

Cancellation Effective Date

Cancellation becomes effective according to the terms of the customer's subscription agreement.

Typical scenarios include:

Monthly Subscriptions

Cancellation generally becomes effective at the end of the current billing period.

Service remains available until the subscription period expires.

Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscriptions generally remain active until the end of the contracted subscription term unless otherwise specified in the agreement.

Enterprise Agreements

Enterprise agreements follow the termination provisions defined in the executed contract.

Auto-Renewal

Many Work-A-Beez subscriptions renew automatically.

To prevent automatic renewal, customers should submit a cancellation request before the renewal date in accordance with their subscription agreement.

Once a renewal has been processed, the subscription remains subject to the applicable agreement and Refund Policy.

Billing After Cancellation

Following cancellation:

  • No additional recurring subscription charges will be generated after the cancellation becomes effective.
  • Charges incurred before the effective cancellation date remain payable.
  • Professional services already delivered remain billable.
  • Outstanding invoices continue to be due according to their payment terms.

Cancellation does not automatically cancel unpaid financial obligations.

Access Following Cancellation

Until the effective cancellation date:

  • Authorized users retain normal access.
  • Administrators may continue managing the account.
  • Data remains available.

After cancellation becomes effective:

  • User access is disabled.
  • Administrative access is removed.
  • API credentials may be revoked.
  • Integrations may stop functioning.
  • Scheduled background processes may cease.

Customer Data

Customer data remains the property of the customer, subject to applicable laws and the Work-A-Beez Terms of Service.

Examples of customer data include:

  • Employee records
  • Schedules
  • Timecards
  • Leave balances
  • Reports
  • Uploaded documents
  • Configuration settings

Customers are encouraged to retrieve any required information before cancellation becomes effective.

Data Retention

Following cancellation, customer data may be retained for a limited period in accordance with:

  • Applicable laws
  • Contractual obligations
  • Security policies
  • Disaster recovery requirements
  • Backup retention schedules

After applicable retention periods expire, customer data may be securely deleted or anonymized.

Work-A-Beez does not guarantee indefinite storage of inactive customer data.

Data Export

Before cancellation becomes effective, organizations are encouraged to export any information they wish to retain.

Examples include:

  • Employee records
  • Payroll exports
  • Timekeeping history
  • Leave records
  • Reports
  • Audit logs
  • Scheduling data

Customers are responsible for verifying exported data before account closure.

Outstanding Financial Obligations

Cancellation does not eliminate responsibility for:

  • Outstanding invoices
  • Professional services
  • Approved implementation work
  • Consulting engagements
  • Applicable taxes
  • Contractual obligations

Payments remain subject to the original payment terms.

Reactivation

Organizations wishing to resume service after cancellation should contact Customer Success.

Depending on the length of time since cancellation, reactivation may require:

  • Identity verification
  • New subscription agreement
  • New implementation activities
  • Data restoration (if available)
  • Updated pricing

Restoration of previously deleted customer data cannot be guaranteed.

Customer Responsibilities

Before cancelling, organizations should:

  • Notify internal stakeholders.
  • Export required business records.
  • Download invoices and reports.
  • Remove API integrations if appropriate.
  • Verify billing information.
  • Ensure outstanding obligations are understood.
  • Review contractual commitments.

Proper planning helps ensure a smooth transition.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel at any time?

Cancellation requests may be submitted at any time. The effective cancellation date depends on your subscription agreement and billing cycle.

Will I receive a refund if I cancel early?

Refund eligibility is governed by the Work-A-Beez Refund Policy and any applicable contractual agreement.

Can I continue using Work-A-Beez after requesting cancellation?

Yes.

Access generally remains available until the effective cancellation date unless otherwise specified.

What happens to my employee data?

Customer data is retained according to applicable legal requirements, contractual obligations, and Work-A-Beez data retention policies.

Organizations should export any required information before service ends.

Can I reactivate my subscription later?

Yes.

Organizations may request reactivation by contacting Customer Success. Availability of previous configuration or data depends on applicable retention periods and restoration capabilities.

Does cancelling stop automatic renewal?

Yes.

Once a cancellation request has been processed according to your subscription agreement, future automatic renewals will not occur.
