This guide explains how Work-A-Beez subscriptions are structured, how licensing works, available billing options, optional services, and frequently requested pricing information.

Because every organization has unique workforce management requirements, some pricing elements may be customized based on deployment size, feature requirements, and professional services.

Overview

Work-A-Beez is offered as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) subscription.

Subscriptions provide access to:

  • Cloud-hosted platform
  • Regular software updates
  • Security updates
  • Product enhancements
  • Customer support
  • Documentation
  • Knowledge Base
  • Service monitoring

Customers do not purchase software licenses outright. Instead, they subscribe to the platform for the duration of their agreement.

Subscription Model

Organizations subscribe based on factors such as:

  • Number of licensed users
  • Employee population
  • Enabled modules
  • Selected support plan
  • Contract duration
  • Optional add-on services

Your subscription agreement defines the services included in your plan. See the Pricing page for current published plans.

Licensing

Work-A-Beez licenses are assigned at the organization level.

Licensing may include users such as:

  • Employees
  • Supervisors
  • Managers
  • HR personnel
  • Payroll administrators
  • System administrators

Licenses are intended for named users and may not be shared unless explicitly permitted by your subscription agreement.

Billing Options

Work-A-Beez supports flexible billing arrangements.

Common billing options include:

Monthly Billing

  • Predictable monthly payments
  • Ideal for growing organizations
  • Automatic recurring billing

Annual Billing

  • One annual invoice
  • Simplified budgeting
  • May include discounted pricing depending on the subscription agreement

Organizations should consult their agreement for available billing options.

What's Included

Every subscription includes core platform services such as:

  • Secure cloud hosting
  • Workforce management platform
  • User authentication
  • Standard reporting
  • Software maintenance
  • Security updates
  • Regular feature enhancements
  • Technical documentation

Included features vary by subscription tier.

Optional Add-On Services

Organizations may purchase additional services, including:

  • Premium support
  • Additional storage
  • Custom integrations
  • Professional consulting
  • Dedicated implementation assistance
  • Training packages

Availability depends on subscription level and region.

Professional Services

Professional Services are available for organizations requiring additional assistance.

Examples include:

  • Implementation consulting
  • Data migration
  • Configuration assistance
  • Custom workflows
  • Solution architecture
  • Business process consulting

Professional Services are typically quoted separately from software subscriptions.

Implementation Services

Implementation services may include:

  • Project planning
  • Discovery workshops
  • Organizational configuration
  • Employee data migration
  • Payroll setup
  • Security configuration
  • Go-live assistance

Implementation timelines vary according to project scope.

Training Services

Training options may include:

  • Administrator training
  • Employee training
  • Manager training
  • Live virtual sessions
  • Self-paced learning resources

Training availability may depend on the selected subscription or service package.

Storage & Data Retention

Subscriptions include secure cloud storage for operational data.

Storage limits, archival policies, and data retention periods depend on:

  • Subscription plan
  • Organizational policies
  • Compliance requirements
  • Contract terms

Customers requiring additional storage should contact their account representative.

Subscription Changes

Organizations may request changes to their subscription.

Examples include:

  • Adding users
  • Removing users
  • Upgrading plans
  • Purchasing additional modules
  • Adding support services

Changes may become effective immediately or at the next billing cycle, depending on contractual terms.

Taxes & Fees

Subscription pricing may exclude:

  • Sales tax
  • Value-added tax (VAT)
  • Goods and Services Tax (GST)
  • Other applicable governmental fees

Taxes are calculated according to applicable laws and the customer's billing location.

Renewals

Subscriptions automatically renew unless otherwise specified in the service agreement.

Prior to renewal, organizations should review:

  • User counts
  • Subscription tier
  • Optional services
  • Billing information
  • Contract terms

Renewal notices are provided in accordance with applicable agreements and local regulations.

Enterprise Pricing

Large organizations may qualify for customized pricing based on:

  • Workforce size
  • Number of locations
  • Geographic distribution
  • Support requirements
  • Compliance needs
  • Professional services

Enterprise pricing is provided through a customized quotation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every plan includes a free 90-day trial. See the Pricing page for details.

Can I upgrade at any time?

Yes.

Organizations may request subscription upgrades during their contract term, subject to applicable pricing and agreement terms.

Can I reduce my licenses?

License reductions may be available at renewal or as otherwise provided in your subscription agreement.

Do unused licenses roll over?

No.

Unused licenses generally do not carry forward or generate billing credits unless specifically stated in your agreement.

Are software updates included?

Yes.

Product updates, security patches, and maintenance releases are included throughout your active subscription.

Are implementation services included?

Implementation services may be included in certain subscription packages or purchased separately.

Can pricing change?

Pricing may change upon renewal or when purchasing additional products or services. Customers will be notified according to the terms of their agreement.
