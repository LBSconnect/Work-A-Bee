Overview

Work-A-Beez training is designed to help users:

  • Learn platform features
  • Complete daily tasks efficiently
  • Improve workforce management processes
  • Configure the platform correctly
  • Reduce onboarding time
  • Increase user adoption

Training materials are updated regularly to reflect new platform capabilities and best practices.

Learning Philosophy

Training follows a progressive learning model.

Users are encouraged to:

  1. Learn the basics.
  2. Practice in a safe environment.
  3. Apply knowledge to real business processes.
  4. Continue learning as new features are released.

This approach helps users gain confidence while minimizing operational disruption.

Learning Paths

Training is organized by user role.

Available learning paths include:

  • New Users

  • IT Administrators

  • Implementation Teams

Each path focuses on the features and responsibilities relevant to that role.

New User Training

Recommended for all first-time users.

Topics include:

  • Signing in
  • Password management
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
  • Updating personal information
  • Navigating the dashboard
  • Viewing schedules
  • Clocking in and out
  • Requesting leave
  • Viewing notifications
  • Accessing help resources

Estimated completion time:

30-60 minutes

Self-Paced Learning

Self-paced learning allows users to complete training according to their own schedule.

Instructor-Led Training

Organizations may choose instructor-led training sessions.

Available formats may include:

  • Virtual classroom
  • Live webinars
  • On-site training
  • Administrator workshops
  • Implementation training

Instructor-led training provides opportunities for live demonstrations and questions.

Hands-On Practice

Practical experience is one of the most effective ways to learn.

Organizations are encouraged to provide a non-production environment where users can safely practice:

  • Scheduling
  • Time entry
  • Leave requests
  • Report generation
  • User management
  • Administrative configuration

Practice helps reinforce classroom and self-paced learning.

Training Best Practices

To maximize learning effectiveness:

  • Complete training in order.
  • Practice using sample data.
  • Encourage hands-on experience.
  • Review documentation alongside videos.
  • Stay current with product releases.
  • Participate in refresher training.
  • Share knowledge within your organization.

Continuous learning helps organizations realize the full value of the platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does training take?

Training duration depends on the user's role.

Typical estimates range from 30 minutes for basic users to 10 hours for administrators.

Is training required?

Organizations determine their own training requirements. Work-A-Beez strongly recommends that all users complete the appropriate learning path.

Are videos available?

Yes.

A library of instructional videos is available to supplement written documentation.

Is instructor-led training available?

Yes.

Organizations may request virtual or instructor-led training sessions through Customer Success.

Are training materials updated?

Yes.

Training resources are reviewed and updated to reflect new platform features and enhancements.

Where should new users start?

New users should begin with the New User Training path before progressing to role-specific learning materials.
