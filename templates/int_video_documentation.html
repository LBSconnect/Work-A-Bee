Welcome to the Work-A-Beez Video Documentation Center.

The Work-A-Beez video library provides visual, step-by-step instruction for every major feature of the platform. Videos are organized by role, feature area, and skill level to help users quickly find the information they need.

Videos complement the written documentation and are intended to accelerate onboarding, improve adoption, and reinforce best practices.

Overview

The Work-A-Beez video library is designed to support users at every stage of their learning journey.

Video content includes:

  • Product introductions
  • Feature demonstrations
  • Step-by-step tutorials
  • Administrative walkthroughs
  • Best practice guidance
  • Troubleshooting assistance
  • Release feature overviews
  • Live webinar recordings

Videos are updated regularly to reflect platform enhancements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are videos free?

Yes.

Instructional videos are available as part of the Work-A-Beez documentation and training resources.

Do videos replace written documentation?

No.

Videos are designed to complement the written documentation by providing visual demonstrations and guided walkthroughs.

Are videos updated?

Yes.

Videos are reviewed and updated whenever significant product changes occur.

Can organizations request custom training videos?

Enterprise customers may request customized training or implementation workshops through Customer Success.

Are captions available?

Where supported, videos include closed captions to improve accessibility.

Can videos be downloaded?

Download availability depends on the hosting platform and organizational permissions.
