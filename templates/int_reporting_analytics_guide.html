Welcome to the Work-A-Beez Reporting & Analytics Guide.

Reporting and analytics transform workforce data into actionable insights that help organizations improve productivity, control labor costs, maintain compliance, and make informed business decisions.

This guide explains how to access dashboards, run reports, build custom reports, schedule report delivery, and interpret key workforce metrics.

Overview

Work-A-Beez provides powerful reporting capabilities designed for organizations of all sizes.

Reports enable organizations to:

  • Monitor workforce activity
  • Analyze labor costs
  • Review attendance trends
  • Track overtime
  • Improve scheduling efficiency
  • Support payroll
  • Demonstrate compliance
  • Measure organizational performance

Most reports can be viewed on-screen or exported for external analysis.

Reporting Architecture

Work-A-Beez reporting is built upon operational data collected from:

  • Employee profiles
  • Time entries
  • Schedules
  • Leave requests
  • Departments
  • Locations
  • Payroll exports
  • User activity
  • Audit logs

Reports use the latest available approved data unless otherwise specified.

Dashboard Overview

The Analytics Dashboard provides a real-time overview of workforce activity.

Typical dashboard widgets include:

  • Employees Clocked In
  • Scheduled Employees
  • Attendance Rate
  • Overtime Hours
  • Open Shifts
  • Pending Leave Requests
  • Labor Cost Today
  • Missed Punches
  • Payroll Status
  • Compliance Alerts

Administrators may configure dashboard layouts based on their role.

Timecard Report

Schedule Report

  • Coverage gaps

Leave Report

Payroll Export Report

Displays:

Custom Reports

Administrators can build custom reports using the Report Builder.

Available data fields include:

  • Employee
  • Department
  • Manager
  • Job Title
  • Location
  • Shift
  • Time Entry
  • Payroll
  • Leave
  • Schedule
  • Custom Fields

Custom reports can be saved and reused.

Report Filters

Reports may be filtered using one or more criteria.

Common filters include:

  • Date Range
  • Department
  • Location
  • Employee
  • Manager
  • Job Title
  • Employment Status
  • Shift Type
  • Schedule
  • Payroll Period

Multiple filters may be combined to refine report results.

Saved Reports

Frequently used reports may be saved for future use.

Saved reports preserve:

  • Selected columns
  • Filters
  • Sorting
  • Grouping
  • Calculated fields

Only authorized users may modify shared reports.

Scheduled Reports

Reports can be generated automatically.

Scheduling options include:

  • Daily
  • Weekly
  • Monthly
  • Quarterly

Reports may be delivered by:

  • Email
  • Secure download
  • Shared reporting portal

Administrators should avoid scheduling large reports during peak operational periods.

Export Options

Supported export formats include:

  • CSV
  • Excel (.xlsx)
  • PDF

Exports maintain report formatting where applicable.

Large reports may require additional processing time.

Workforce KPIs

Common workforce metrics include:

Attendance Rate

Percentage of scheduled employees who reported to work.

Overtime Hours

Total overtime worked during the selected reporting period.

Labor Cost

Combined wage cost associated with worked hours.

Schedule Coverage

Percentage of required shifts successfully filled.

Leave Utilization

Percentage of accrued leave that has been used.

Employee Turnover

Percentage of employees leaving the organization during a reporting period.

Average Hours Worked

Average hours worked per employee.

Missed Punch Rate

Percentage of time entries requiring correction.

Labor Cost Analytics

Labor cost reporting enables organizations to monitor workforce expenses.

Available metrics include:

  • Labor cost by department
  • Labor cost by location
  • Labor cost by manager
  • Labor cost by employee
  • Overtime expense
  • Budget variance
  • Cost per labor hour

These reports support workforce planning and budgeting.

Attendance Analytics

Attendance reports help identify workforce trends.

Common metrics include:

  • Tardiness
  • Early departures
  • No-call/no-show events
  • Missed punches
  • Absence frequency
  • Attendance percentages

Managers can use attendance trends to improve workforce reliability.

Scheduling Analytics

Scheduling reports help optimize staffing levels.

Examples include:

  • Shift coverage
  • Open shifts
  • Shift swaps
  • Schedule adherence
  • Understaffing
  • Overstaffing

Analytics assist managers in balancing labor costs with operational demand.

PTO & Leave Analytics

Leave reports include:

  • PTO balances
  • Vacation usage
  • Sick leave
  • Holiday utilization
  • Leave trends
  • Department leave summaries

Managers can identify periods of increased leave activity for workforce planning.

Compliance Reporting

Compliance reports support organizational and regulatory requirements.

Examples include:

  • Overtime compliance
  • Break compliance
  • Rest period compliance
  • Maximum hours worked
  • Timekeeping exceptions
  • Audit history

Compliance reports should be reviewed regularly to identify potential issues before they become violations.

Executive dashboards summarize organizational performance through high-level metrics.

Typical executive indicators include:

  • Total Workforce
  • Active Employees
  • Labor Cost
  • Overtime Percentage
  • Attendance Rate
  • Leave Utilization
  • Payroll Status
  • Department Performance
  • Workforce Trends

Executives may customize dashboards to align with organizational goals.

Best Practices

To maximize reporting effectiveness:

  • Use consistent date ranges.
  • Verify report filters before exporting.
  • Schedule recurring reports.
  • Archive historical reports.
  • Limit access to sensitive reports.
  • Review labor trends regularly.
  • Monitor compliance reports routinely.

Organizations should establish reporting standards to ensure consistent interpretation across departments.

Troubleshooting

Missing Data

Verify:

  • Date filters
  • Employee permissions
  • Approved time entries
  • Data synchronization status

Incorrect Totals

Review:

  • Report filters
  • Payroll approval status
  • Timecard approvals
  • Overtime rules

Export Failures

Confirm:

  • User permissions
  • Browser compatibility
  • File size limitations
  • Available storage space

Scheduled Reports Not Delivered

Check:

  • Recipient email addresses
  • Delivery schedule
  • Spam filtering
  • Report ownership
