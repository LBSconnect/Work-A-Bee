Work-A-Beez Product Overview

Last updated: {{ updated.strftime('%B %d, %Y') }}

Welcome to Work-A-Beez, a modern workforce management platform designed to help organizations simplify employee management, automate administrative tasks, and improve operational efficiency.

Work-A-Beez provides an all-in-one solution for managing employee timekeeping, scheduling, leave requests, communication, workforce analytics, and administrative workflows from a single secure platform.

Whether your organization employs five people or five thousand, Work-A-Beez helps businesses reduce paperwork, improve accountability, and give employees the tools they need to manage their workday efficiently.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple:

Help businesses spend less time managing employees and more time growing their business.

We accomplish this by replacing spreadsheets, paper forms, disconnected software, and manual processes with one centralized workforce management platform.

Core Platform

The Work-A-Beez platform consists of multiple integrated modules.

Employee Timekeeping

Track employee work hours with accurate clock-in and clock-out functionality.

Features include:

  • Web clock
  • Mobile clock
  • GPS verification (optional)
  • Geofencing
  • Shift validation
  • Break tracking
  • Missed punch requests
  • Overtime monitoring
  • Payroll-ready timecards

Scheduling

Create and manage employee work schedules quickly.

Managers can:

  • Create schedules
  • Publish schedules
  • Assign shifts
  • Copy previous schedules
  • Manage recurring schedules
  • Monitor coverage
  • Fill open shifts

Employees can:

  • View schedules
  • Receive schedule updates
  • Accept available shifts
  • Request shift changes

Paid Time Off (PTO)

Employees can submit leave requests directly through the platform.

Supported leave types include:

  • Vacation
  • Sick Leave
  • Personal Leave
  • Jury Duty
  • Bereavement
  • Military Leave
  • Unpaid Leave
  • Custom Leave Types

Managers receive approval requests and employees are automatically notified of decisions.

Shift Marketplace

The Shift Marketplace allows employees to:

  • Offer shifts
  • Request shift swaps
  • Pick up available shifts
  • Receive manager approval

This helps organizations maintain staffing levels while reducing administrative workload.

Employee Self-Service

Employees have access to a personalized dashboard where they can:

  • Clock in and out
  • View schedules
  • Request PTO
  • Swap shifts
  • Update availability
  • View announcements
  • Access company documents
  • Review attendance
  • View worked hours
  • Manage profile information

Workforce Communication

Improve communication between employees and management.

Communication tools include:
