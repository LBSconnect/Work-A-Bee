Welcome to the Work-A-Beez Feature Overview.

This document provides a high-level summary of every major capability available within the Work-A-Beez Workforce Management Platform. Each feature is designed to help organizations improve productivity, reduce administrative overhead, and provide employees with a modern self-service experience.

Detailed instructions for each feature are available in their respective user guides and administrator documentation.

Employee Dashboard

Every employee receives a personalized dashboard after signing in.

The dashboard provides immediate access to:

  • Clock In / Clock Out
  • Today's Schedule
  • Hours Worked
  • Weekly Time Summary
  • PTO Balance
  • Announcements
  • Notifications
  • Shift Requests
  • Messages
  • Upcoming Events

The dashboard is designed to minimize clicks and provide employees with the information they need throughout their workday.

Timekeeping

Work-A-Beez provides accurate, payroll-ready employee time tracking.

Features

  • Clock In
  • Clock Out
  • Break Tracking
  • Meal Period Tracking
  • Shift Validation
  • Missed Punch Requests
  • Manual Time Adjustments (Manager Approval)
  • Automatic Hour Calculations
  • Overtime Tracking
  • Holiday Hours
  • PTO Hours
  • Attendance Monitoring

Optional Features

  • GPS Verification
  • Geofencing
  • IP Address Restrictions
  • Device Restrictions
  • Biometric Authentication (Future)

Scheduling

Managers can efficiently create and maintain employee schedules.

Features

  • Weekly Scheduling
  • Monthly Scheduling
  • Shift Templates
  • Recurring Shifts
  • Open Shifts
  • Drag-and-Drop Scheduling
  • Department Scheduling
  • Multi-location Scheduling
  • Conflict Detection
  • Schedule Publishing

Employees receive automatic notifications whenever schedules are updated.

Shift Marketplace

Employees can exchange or claim available shifts without manual coordination.

Features

  • Offer Shift
  • Request Shift Swap
  • Claim Open Shift
  • Manager Approval Workflow
  • Swap History
  • Eligibility Validation

Benefits include:

  • Reduced scheduling conflicts
  • Improved staffing coverage
  • Increased employee flexibility

Leave & PTO Management

Employees can request time off electronically.

Supported leave types include:

  • Vacation
  • Sick Leave
  • Personal Leave
  • Jury Duty
  • Military Leave
  • Bereavement
  • Family Leave
  • Unpaid Leave
  • Custom Leave Types

Managers receive approval requests directly within the platform.

Employees are automatically notified when requests are approved or denied.

Employee Availability

Employees can maintain their preferred work availability.

Examples include:

  • Morning Availability
  • Evening Availability
  • Weekend Availability
  • Temporary Schedule Changes
  • Seasonal Availability

Managers can reference employee availability while building schedules.

Workforce Communication

Improve collaboration through centralized communication.

Features

  • Company Announcements
  • Department Announcements
  • Team Messages
  • Employee Notifications
  • Policy Acknowledgements
  • Emergency Alerts
  • Reminder Notifications

Employee Directory

Authorized users may search and view employee information.

Information may include:

  • Employee Name
  • Department
  • Job Title
  • Location
  • Supervisor
  • Contact Information
  • Employment Status

Displayed information depends on user permissions.

Company Documents

Organizations can securely distribute important documents.

Examples include:

  • Employee Handbook
  • Safety Policies
  • HR Forms
  • Company Procedures
  • Training Materials
  • Benefit Information

Administrators may require electronic acknowledgement for selected documents.

Reporting & Analytics

Generate workforce reports to support operational decision-making.

Standard reports include:

  • Attendance Summary
  • Hours Worked
  • Overtime Report
  • PTO Usage
  • Payroll Summary
  • Employee Activity
  • Schedule Coverage
  • Labor Cost Analysis
  • Department Reports

Additional custom reports may be available depending on subscription level.

Payroll Support

Work-A-Beez helps organizations prepare payroll efficiently.

Features include:

  • Payroll-ready Timesheets
  • Export Hours
  • Overtime Calculations
  • Holiday Pay Tracking
  • PTO Tracking
  • Approval Workflow

Work-A-Beez does not process payroll unless specifically integrated with a supported payroll provider.

Company Administration

Company Administrators manage organizational settings.

Administrative capabilities include:

  • Company Profile
  • Locations
  • Departments
  • Job Titles
  • Work Rules
  • Holiday Calendars
  • Leave Policies
  • Branding
  • Notification Settings
  • Subscription Management

User Management

Administrators control user accounts and permissions.

Supported functions include:

  • Invite Employees
  • Disable Users
  • Reset Passwords
  • Assign Roles
  • Configure Permissions
  • Manage Departments
  • Bulk Import Employees

Notifications

The platform automatically delivers important notifications.

Examples include:

  • Schedule Published
  • Schedule Changed
  • PTO Approved
  • PTO Denied
  • Shift Swap Requested
  • Shift Approved
  • Missed Punch Submitted
  • New Announcement
  • Password Changed

Notifications may be delivered by:

  • In-App Notification
  • Email
  • SMS (Optional)
  • Push Notification (Future)

Security Features

Security is integrated throughout the platform.

Features include:

  • Secure Login
  • Password Requirements
  • Multi-Factor Authentication
  • Session Timeout
  • Role-Based Permissions
  • Audit Logging
  • Encrypted Data Transmission
  • Secure Cloud Infrastructure

Audit Logs

Administrative actions are recorded to support accountability.

Examples include:

  • Login Activity
  • Password Changes
  • Permission Changes
  • Employee Updates
  • Schedule Modifications
  • Timecard Edits
  • PTO Approvals
  • Billing Changes

Audit logs assist organizations with compliance and internal investigations.

Mobile Experience

Work-A-Beez is designed for desktop and mobile users.

Employees can:

  • Clock In
  • View Schedules
  • Submit PTO
  • Swap Shifts
  • Read Announcements
  • View Notifications
  • Update Availability

Responsive design ensures a consistent experience across supported devices.
