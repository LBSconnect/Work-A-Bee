General Questions

What is Work-A-Beez?

Work-A-Beez is a cloud-based Workforce Management (WFM) platform that helps organizations manage employees, schedules, attendance, leave, payroll preparation, compliance, reporting, and workforce operations from a single application.

Who is Work-A-Beez designed for?

Work-A-Beez supports organizations of all sizes including:

  • Small businesses
  • Mid-sized companies
  • Enterprise organizations
  • Healthcare
  • Retail
  • Hospitality
  • Manufacturing
  • Logistics
  • Government
  • Education
  • Professional services

Is Work-A-Beez cloud-based?

Yes.

Work-A-Beez is delivered as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform that is securely hosted in the cloud.

No local software installation is required for normal use.

Do employees need to install software?

No.

Employees may use:

  • Supported web browsers
  • The Work-A-Beez mobile application (where available)

Features

What can employees do?

Employees can:

  • Clock in and out
  • View schedules
  • Request leave
  • View PTO balances
  • Receive notifications
  • Update personal information (if permitted)
  • Access company announcements

What can managers do?

Managers can:

  • Create schedules
  • Approve leave
  • Approve timecards
  • Monitor attendance
  • Fill open shifts
  • Review reports
  • Manage teams

Does Work-A-Beez support mobile devices?

Yes.

Supported mobile applications allow users to:

  • Clock in/out
  • View schedules
  • Receive notifications
  • Request leave
  • Approve requests (where authorized)

Can Work-A-Beez support multiple locations?

Yes.

Organizations can manage:

  • Multiple offices
  • Stores
  • Departments
  • Regions
  • Business units

from a single platform.

Security

Is my data encrypted?

Yes.

Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption technologies.

Does Work-A-Beez support Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)?

Yes.

Organizations may require MFA to provide an additional layer of account security.

Can I use Single Sign-On (SSO)?

Yes.

Supported authentication methods may include:

  • Microsoft Entra ID
  • Google Workspace
  • SAML-compatible identity providers
  • OAuth providers

Availability depends on your subscription and configuration.

Who can access my organization's data?

Access is controlled through Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).

Users can access only the information permitted by their assigned roles and permissions.

Frequently Asked Troubleshooting Questions

I forgot my password.

Use the Forgot Password link on the sign-in page or contact your organization's administrator if additional assistance is required.

I am locked out of my account.

Wait for the lockout period to expire or contact your administrator to unlock your account.

I didn't receive my verification code.

Verify:

  • Your contact information is correct.
  • Spam or junk folders.
  • Mobile signal or email availability.

If the issue persists, contact your administrator.

Why can't I clock in?

Possible reasons include:

  • You are outside the approved location.
  • Your scheduled shift has not started.
  • GPS is disabled.
  • Mobile permissions are restricted.
  • Your organization has disabled mobile clock-in.

My schedule is missing.

Verify that:

  • The schedule has been published.
  • You are viewing the correct date.
  • Your manager has assigned shifts.
